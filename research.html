ARCUS-X

Adaptive Reasoning Compliance Under Sequential eXecution for Long-Horizon Language Model Evaluation

ARCUS-X is a novel benchmark that evaluates how reliably large language models maintain state and execute sequential transformations over extended reasoning horizons.

OptiTRNG

A biological true random number generator that derives physical entropy from optical measurements of sea urchin sperm motion

This project investigates micro-scale biological kinematics to generate cryptographically secure true entropy, processing live-captured optical tracking metrics into robust non-deterministic bitstreams.
