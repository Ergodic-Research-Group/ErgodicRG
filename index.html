Independent research in computation, physics, biology, and intelligent systems.

Ergodic Research Group (ERG) is an independent research initiative focused on developing original interdisciplinary technologies at the intersection of software engineering, applied physics, computational biology, cryptography, and artificial intelligence. The group serves as a home for long-term research projects, open-source software, and intellectual property development.

Mission

The mission of Ergodic Research Group is to investigate scientific questions, conventional or unconventional, through rigorous experimentation and software engineering. Projects emphasize reproducibility, interdisciplinary collaboration, and the development of technologies that extend beyond traditional disciplinary boundaries.
